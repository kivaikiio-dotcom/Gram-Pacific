    Questions owners ask us

    Frequently asked questions

    How much does a full-time remote employee cost?
    Standard roles are a flat $3,000 per month for a dedicated full-time employee working 40 hours per week on your schedule. Software developers start at $3,800. Team Scale and Prepay Advantage discounts stack up to 20% off, bringing a standard seat as low as $2,400/month for larger, committed teams.
    Where are your professionals based?
    Our professionals work from Gram Pacific's international delivery hubs, where we recruit from deep, university-educated, English-fluent talent markets. Every employee is directly engaged and managed by Gram Pacific, works exclusively for your business, and operates fully on your time zone. We're happy to walk through our delivery model, security practices, and compliance framework on a call.
    Is this like hiring a virtual assistant?
    It's what people hope a VA will be. Instead of a shared assistant or a task subscription, you get one named, full-time employee dedicated to your business, plus an Operations Manager supervising their work daily. And it's not limited to assistants: bookkeeping, accounting, customer service, marketing, healthcare admin, and software development are all on the menu.
    What if it's not a fit?
    Our replacement guarantee provides a vetted, trained substitute within ten business days at no additional cost. Contracts carry a simple 30-day notice period, and confidentiality clauses are standard in every agreement.
    How do the discounts combine?
    Two ladders stack: Team Scale (5% at 2–4 employees, 10% at 5–9, 15% at 10+) and Prepay Advantage (3% for 3 months upfront, 6% for 6 months, 10% for 12 months), capped at 20% combined. A referral that becomes a placement also earns you a $1,000 invoice credit.
    Who handles payroll, equipment, and benefits?
    We do, entirely. Your flat fee covers the employee's salary, statutory benefits, laptop, connectivity stipend, training, and management. You receive one invoice a month and nothing else.
    Is my business data safe?
    Security is contractual and technical: confidentiality clauses in every agreement, least-privilege access to your systems, managed devices with VPN, and an ISO 27001 certification program underway. Client-specific data processing agreements are available on request.

    Before you ask

    Talent FAQ

    Is this freelancing? Will I bid for jobs?
    No. You become a salaried, full-time professional in Gram Pacific's talent pool. We find the clients, negotiate the contracts, and assign you to one dedicated client. You never bid, never chase invoices, and never compete on price.
    What are the working hours?
    Placements run 40 hours per week aligned to the client's US, Canadian, or Australian time zone. For East Africa that typically means afternoon-to-midnight shifts; a night-shift allowance applies where relevant, and your Ops Manager plans sustainable coverage.
    Do I get paid during training or on the bench?
    GP Academy comes with a training stipend. Once you pass and join the bench, you're salaried while awaiting placement — for strong candidates in high-demand tracks, usually a matter of days.
    Is there any fee to apply or train?
    Never. Application, assessment, and GP Academy are free, and training is stipended. Anyone charging money in Gram Pacific's name is committing fraud; report it to talent@grampacific.com.
    Can I grow into management?
    Yes, and we promote from within by policy. Operations Managers, who each supervise 10–20 professionals, are recruited primarily from our senior specialist ranks.
